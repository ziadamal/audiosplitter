## Quick Deploy (5 Minutes Total)

### Step 1: Upload These Files to GitHub (2 minutes)

1. Go to your repository: **https://github.com/ziadamal/audiosplitter**
2. Click "Add file" ‚Üí "Upload files"
3. Drag these 5 files from this folder:
   - `render.yaml`
   - `DEPLOY_NOW.md`
   - `DEPLOYMENT_GUIDE.md`
   - `QUICK_START.md`
   - `.env.example`
4. Scroll down and click "Commit changes"

### Step 2: Get Hugging Face Token (2 minutes)

1. Create account at **https://huggingface.co/join**
2. Accept model terms:
   - https://huggingface.co/pyannote/speaker-diarization-3.1 (click "Agree")
   - https://huggingface.co/pyannote/segmentation-3.0 (click "Agree")
3. Get token at **https://huggingface.co/settings/tokens**
   - Click "New token"
   - Name: `voxsplit`
   - Click "Generate"
   - **COPY THE TOKEN**

### Step 3: Deploy to Render.com (1 minute)

1. Go to **https://render.com/register** and sign up with GitHub
2. Click this magic link: **https://render.com/deploy?repo=https://github.com/ziadamal/audiosplitter**
3. Paste your Hugging Face token when asked
4. Click "Create Web Service"
5. Wait 5-10 minutes

### Step 4: Done! üéâ

When you see "Live" with a green dot, click the URL and your website is online!
