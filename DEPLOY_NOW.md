## Step 1: Get Your Hugging Face Token (3 minutes)

1. Go to **https://huggingface.co/join** and create a free account
2. Accept model terms:
   - Visit https://huggingface.co/pyannote/speaker-diarization-3.1 and click "Agree"
   - Visit https://huggingface.co/pyannote/segmentation-3.0 and click "Agree"
3. Get your token:
   - Go to https://huggingface.co/settings/tokens
   - Click "New token"
   - Name it `voxsplit`
   - Click "Generate token"
   - **COPY THIS TOKEN** - you'll need it in Step 3!

## Step 2: Create Render Account (1 minute)

1. Go to **https://render.com/register**
2. Click "Sign up with GitHub"
3. Authorize Render to access your GitHub

## Step 3: Deploy with One Click! (1 minute)

1. Click this link: **https://render.com/deploy?repo=https://github.com/ziadamal/audiosplitter**

2. Render will ask for your Hugging Face token:
   - Paste the token you copied in Step 1
   - Click "Apply"

3. Click "Create Web Service"

4. Wait 5-10 minutes while Render builds your app

5. When you see "Live" with a green dot, **your website is online!**

## Step 4: Use Your Website!

Click on the URL that Render gives you (something like `https://voxsplit-frontend.onrender.com`)
